🎵 Japanese words have a melody!
Every syllable is either High or Low. Same sounds + different melody = totally different word 😲
The mic can't hear melody (yet 😅), so here's the trick: tap 🔊 to hear the word in a short sentence, listen closely, and copy the tune — karaoke style 🎤
Don't stress: context saves you 99% of the time. Copying the melody just makes you sound extra natural!
